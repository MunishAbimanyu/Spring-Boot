About Spirulina
Spirulina is a type of blue-green algae that is rich in protein, vitamins, minerals, carotenoids,
and antioxidants that can help protect cells from damage. It contains nutrients,
including B complex vitamins, beta-carotene, vitamin E, manganese, zinc,
copper, iron, selenium, and gamma linolenic acid (an essential fatty acid).
